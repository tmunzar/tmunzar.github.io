Stardog Studio

Over the course of interning as a UX Designer at Stardog Union during the summer of 2018, I was put in charge of helping redesign Stardog Studio, an IDE for querying graph databases using SPARQL queries.

Initial interview notes

What was unique about this challenge was that I was brought in as a UX intern in a company where they've never had any sort of user experience module in their organization.

Another challenge for this experience was that my access to end-users was non-existent due to a myriad of reason. I had to the majortiy of my user-research within the company itself. So I started by interviwing and gathering data from the customer service and sales departments of the company.

Initial sketches

I sketched designs using that data, and validated them by making Sketch/Invision prototypes but presenting and testing them with the appropriate in-company stakeholders. I was later granted access to two end-users to validate the design decisions.

I then worked closely with the front-end engineering team to implement these design decisions. I engineered in an ElectronJS/React/Redux environment, and used BlueprintJS & SASS to implement the styling.
