Jellyfish

Jellyfish, the small but successful design firm in Pakistan that I ran for five years with the intention of filling the gap of design thought within the country’s growing web and digital industry. This is the mission that fueled this firm over these years, and we worked with clients to build a user-first experience for their products in a place where wireframes are seldom heard of.

To emphasize the design first approach, the design was made with a heavy emphasis on typography.
