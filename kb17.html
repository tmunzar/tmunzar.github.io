Karachi Bienalle 2017

A website for the 2017 version of the annual visual arts festival, the Karachi Bienalle. A one place stop to view all the artists participating, view the festival schedule, and to host 3D archives of the exhbitions.

Design screens

This website was developed with a close collaboration with graphic designer Samya Arif. One of the three collaborations I have done with her. It's a website to showcase the artists, venues and projects of the Karachi Bienalle 2017 artist festival.

The website was implemented using Zurb Foundation 6 for the front-end and powered by a CockpitCMS backend.
