RMI

Rehman Medical Institue is Peshawar's leading medical college and institute. They wanted a fresh update to their website and I was not only involved in redesigning the look and feel of it, but also wokred to update its code and functionality to make it faster to load and easier to maintain.

Design Screens

I worked closely with a graphic designer to craft the look of the website. I also took lead of the project as its manager. I would lead the frequent meetings with the client to negotiate requirements and pitch design decisions.

A wireframe process was used to ideate the intial design. After the finalized design the webiste was first hi-fi prototyped and the then was implemented using Foundation 6 for its frontend and Sylius to power its backend. Jquery was used for the effects on the website.
