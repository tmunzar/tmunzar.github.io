RMI

Rehman Medical Institue is Peshawar's leading medical college and institute. They wanted a fresh update to their website and we not only redesigned the look and feel of it, but also updated its code and functionality to make it faster to load and easier to maintain.

The design process was a collaboration with a graphic designer. The website was then developed solely by me using TypiCMS and Foundation 6. VueJS was also integrated along with an ElasticSearch module for search indexing.

The website is currently live and accessible.
