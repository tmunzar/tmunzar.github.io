Angry Ballerina

A UK based clothing botique brand, with a focus to transcend bland fashion statements and disrupt the apparel space by engendering a culture of ‘Awearness’.

Wireframe

A user-centered design process using wireframes was used to design a clothing boutique user experience for this website. I worked closely with a graphic designer to craft the experience that suited both the user-base and the client reqirements.

It was then implemented using TypiCMS and Foundation 6. VueJS was also integrated along with an ElasticSearch module for search indexing.
