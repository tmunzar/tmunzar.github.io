About Me

I am a full stack developer with a 7 year experience working in the web design industry. Five of those years were spent running a small web design firm based called Jellyfish in Islamabad, Pakistan. I did my Bsc (Hons) with a major in Computer Engineering from the Lahore University of Management Sciences. I'm currently enrolled in the Human Computer Interaction masters program at iSchool in University of Maryland - College Park.

My interest is in building clutter free, simple and easy to use interfaces. I am especially interested in tech solutions for the third world. I'm on the lookout for internships in the UI/UX industry these days. While the bulk of my hands-on experience has been in the engineering side, I am looking to delve into the design side of things. I believe my work experience of working with close proximity with designers and being heavily involved in the design process equips me to make that jump (not to mention my current graduate studies in HCI). Check out my skillset and portfolio below. Feel free to contact me for any questions relating to my work.
